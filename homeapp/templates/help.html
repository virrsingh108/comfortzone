Help & Support

1. How do I place an order?

Browse products, add to cart, and proceed to checkout. Fill in your details and complete payment.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and COD (Cash on Delivery).

3. What is your return policy?

Items can be returned within 7 days of delivery in original condition. Contact us for a return request.

4. How can I track my order?

Go to the 'Track Your Order' section in the navigation menu.

5. Having login issues?

Check your credentials or reset your password using the "Forgot Password" link.
